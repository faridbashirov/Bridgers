Our Work Process

We know what it takes to create something remarkable. Combining strategic thinking and abilities with impactful execution, we’ll elevate your product/service and help you reach the untapped potential you always planned. The following four stages are at the core of how we work:

  • Analysing Data
  • Building Strategy
  • Executing Campaigns
  • Measuring Results

Data-Driven Business Strategy

We gather data by conducting business diagnostics and Marketing audits, including Company, Customer and Competitive analysis, to understand business interfaces, needs and capabilities to choose the right strategic way.

Obtaining Quality Information

  • Collecting - Surveys. Focus group, A/B testing, Big data,etc.
  • Analyzing - Regression and Conjoint analysis
  • Presenting - We put all findings into a more readable format.

Strategic Business Planning & Roadmap

Starting with developing business models and Strategy formulation, we help you understand the big picture. Everything we do is to find the right business direction; it’s the roadmap that aligns where we’re going (your goals) with how we’ll get there (your marketing activities).

Building Strategy porcess

  • Understanding Business Needs
  • Identifying Opportunities
  • Aligning Goals and Activities

Digital Campaign Execution

We develop action plans, define customer segments, identify value propositions and clarify the right communication channels for all types of business models, and execute digital campaigns to achieve predefined goals.

Some of the channels

  • Google Ads - SEO, Google Ads, Google Local etc.
  • Social Media - Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn etc.
  • Local platforms- Yelp, Angi List

Measuring Marketing Efforts

Measuring the impact of marketing efforts against strategic goals can provide insight into the behaviours of your consumer. It keeps your messaging aligned with your consumers’ values and can even open up a new audience!

  • Improved Efficiency- real-time measurement helps to improve efficiency
  • Better ROI - Acquiring more customers and keeping audience engaged
  • Data-Driven Decisions - data will be used for further projects
