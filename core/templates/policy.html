Privacy Policy

Bridger DS Inc. is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Information Collection
  1. The personal information we collect may include your name, email address, and phone number while you create a form request.
  2. We may also collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser type, and information about your use of our website.
Use of Information
  1. We use your personal information to process your submission, send you meeting requests and blog updates, improve our services, and personalise your experience.
  2. We may also use your information for internal business purposes, such as data analysis and audits.
Disclosure of Information
  1. We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing customer service.
  2. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.
Security of Information
  1. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information.
  2. However, no data transmission over the internet or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.
  3. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please immediately notify us of the problem by contacting us following the “Contact Us” section below.
Changes to Privacy Policy
  1. We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or services.
  2. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our website before the change becomes effective.
Contact Us
  1. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our information practices, please contact us at mydata@bridgerds.com

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected on our website, www.bridgerds.com. By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
